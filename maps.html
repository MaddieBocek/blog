Eldorado Georgetown Planting Map

This is a fairly standard contract map for the Forest Service. For this map, I was given raw data for the outline of the units. I coordinated with the Timber team to digitize, clean up, and then classify units. The map is used for informing prospective contractors of size and location of units, and if they choose to take the contract, the map would be used in Avenza to determine boundaries


Lake Tahoe Basin Motor Vehicle Use Map

This was the first priority during my internship with the Forest Service. MVUMs represent roads and motorized trails for the public to use. It is important that they are accurate and updated for the safety of guest and employees. My role in this map was to meet biweekly with the road and the trail coordinators for the Tahoe Basin and make the spatial edits that they request. Imagery and Lidar data were also used for reference, as well as internal Forest Service tools for quality assurance.
